About

Meet Simon. He is a freelance photographer and digital designer.

His sharp eye and versatility enable him to capture great lifestyle shots from portraits to breath-taking landscapes of rally stages. Through the lens, he uses natural lighting to create visuals that are unique and honest.

Simon splits his time between Seattle to tap into his love for nature, and New York City, where he continues to draw some of his greatest creative inspiration.
